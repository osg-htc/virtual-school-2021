    OSGVS21 Schedule¶

    This page contains schedule details for full, invited participants of the OSG Virtual School 2021, who were selected following a prior application process.

    SOME presentations will presented publicly. For information about access to public lectures and demonstrations, visit the Indico scheduling site; note that public attendance requires free registration on that site.

    Week 1: August 2–6¶

    Note: Lectures and demonstrations this week are repeated each day. The first instance is for invited School participants only, and the second instance will also be open to the general public who have registered in advance. Invited participants may attend one or both times.

    During the times marked as “Staff available”, School staff will be monitoring email and the OSG School Slack for questions. So these are good times to work on School-related activities — such as exercises or applying things to your own work.

    Week 2: August 9–13¶

    The focus of this week is on applying what you have learned (and are learning!) to your own work. Plus, there are opportunities to learn more and a wrap-up session at the end.

    Note: The “Extra Topic” sessions will not repeat — each one is a separate topic.

    15:00–16:00 Extra Topic 2: Containers and GPUs

    Wednesday, August 11¶

    The OSG School Showcase consists of three researchers talking about their science, their computing, and how the process of learning about and using High Throughput Computing transformed their work. For many participants of past Schools, this was a highlight! Please try to arrange your schedule to attend this one-time-only event. There will be time at the end to talk to the presenters.

    Friday, August 13¶

    For our last opportunity to be together, there will be a short wrap-up talk, plus a series of lightning talks from the School participants! Lightning talks are optional but encouraged; they are simply a way to present a little bit about your work and what you accomplished during the School. Details, including how to sign up, will be available during the School.
